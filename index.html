A while back the UK band Radiohead open sourced their House of Cards video data in the form of CSV files (spreadsheet files). The CSV files contain x, y and z coordinates for the points in each frame of the video. These points were captured with a special combination of lasers and sensors (more technically, the video data was collected with LiDAR technology, a “camera” that scans 360° and has 64 lasers).
